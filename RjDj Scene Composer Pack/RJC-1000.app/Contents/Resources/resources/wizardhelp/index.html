Welcome to RJC1000

Create mindblowing listening experiences for smart music players

Thanks for downloading RJC1000. We hope that you will enjoy making Reactive Music as much as we do at RjDj. Please drop us a line at info@rjdj.me and tell us how you get on with it!

RJC1000 is an exciting new tool for creating music for smart music players. Please refer to the online manual or watch the video at the bottom of the screen for detailed information on how to use it. Click here to visit the RJC1000 thread at the RjDj Forum. If you just want to cut to the chase, this help wizard will explain the basics. Enjoy!

With RJC1000 you can:

  • Make your own RjDj scenes in a fun and easy way.
  • Add some reactive magic to your existing music.
  • Release your creation on both the RjDj player for iPhone/iPod Touch and the Rj Voyager for the iPad.
  • Reach out to many new fans on the RjDj network.
  • Discover exciting new ways of making music and unleash your creativity!

Where to start?

  • Prepare samples or loops at 22050hz sample rate and 16bit depth and drag them straight onto a pad.
  • Add some spark to your loops with modules such as World Quantizer which turns sounds from your environment into funky beats! Try out different combinations of mad effects.
  • Reality effects and synths are influenced by your actions! They are controlled by various features of your smart music player such as built in microphone, touchscreen and movement sensors.
  • RJC1000 has 4 pages which listeners can explore in RjDj player or Rj Voyager. Make different arrangements on every page and send them on a journey through your music.
  • When your masterpiece is ready, upload it straight to the RjDj network for others to enjoy.

    • Note: RJC1000 is not a DAW. Use your favourite DAW to prepare material to use in RJC1000!
